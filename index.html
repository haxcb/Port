Once upon a time

there was a young lass named Hasiba. Her thirst for knowledge drove her to seek training from King Simon's highly esteemed scholars. Despite the various disciplines being taught, she quickly fell in love with the ways of the healer and the alchemist. With two disciplines at her disposal, she gained interest in aiding young pages on their quests for knighthood.
