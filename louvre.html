Overview

Louvre Odeum is an immersive museum experience that engages and orients visitors through compelling stories. The platform seamlessly links different artworks together under singular narrative experiences. With a wide variety of unique interactions, the platform allows museum go-ers to view museum artworks in more meaningful ways.

PROJECT GOAL
Reaffirm the Louvre’s core value of promoting meaningful encounters between art collections and the public

Odeum

We brought a small theatre experience into a large museum, breaking up the Louvre into a collection of smaller stories

Design Process

Why Storytelling & Wayfinding?

We identified two glaring issues that detracted from the average visitor’s experience in the Louvre. Firstly, information about artwork was not easily understandable for most pieces beyond the museum’s top three works (ie. Mona Lisa). Secondly, considering the massive size of the Louvre, navigating through the museum was a source of frustration for visitors.

With these two problems in mind, we decided to focus on building an in-museum experience to help visitors better understand pieces through storytelling, and supplemented this journey with an easier to use wayfinding system.
