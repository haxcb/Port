Heroes of the Storm

Global Championship

Engaging Heroes fans with a world class esports experience on the web

Overview

The Heroes of the Storm Global Championship is the premier esports league for Blizzard Entertainment’s team brawling MOBA, Heroes of the Storm. The HGC website serves as a hub for fans of the esport to stay up to date with the league schedule, their favorite teams, epic matches, and meaningful statistics.

PROJECT GOAL
Create a high quality esports experience that inspires fans to play the game

A Closer Look at the Stats

On Team pages, fans can check in on how their favorite team is performing.

On the Hero Stats page, HGC fans can gain insight on which heroes are strong in the current esports met.

Design Process

Why fans love esports

Esports provides fans with an opportunity to engage with the game at a deeper level. Different fans have different motivations that drive them to consume esports.

Player Journey into Esports

With HGC, we focused on helping fans learn about the game and creating opportunities for fans to follow their favorite teams and players closely.

Learn

Improving one’s gameplay is a major motivator for engaging with esports. We addressed this need on the site by focusing on stats that could highlight what the current meta is in HGC. Learning is also addressed through the content strategy of the site with articles covering pro player builds and impressions on new heroes.

Follow

On team pages, we highlight team performance so that fans can gain a deeper understanding of how their favorite teams are doing. We also created a special article template for featured editorial content to help high quality articles on players stand out from standard news and updates.
