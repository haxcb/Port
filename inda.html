An interactive node-based infograph

Overview

The Inda Relationship Visualizer is a tool that plots out the character relationships found in the first book of Inda, by Sherwood Smith. Seeing as the universe is massive and laden with over a hundred named characters, readers can easily lose track of who is who. The tool allows readers to easily search up and identify characters that they come across as they read the book. View it in action ▶

Screenshots

Usage

The infograph allows readers to look up relationship maps relative to every character within the book. Readers can choose the centerpoint via a dropdown menu. Further filtration can be done on the node network, by enabling and disabling relation filters as well as filtering out insignificant characters using the slider. Seeing as character relationships are being plot out, a node network was used as it is the most optimal and intuitive representation.
