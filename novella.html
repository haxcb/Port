A learning management system prototype

Overview

Novella is a mobile application that facilitates the exchange of educational content within an institution. It is designed around a student perspective of what is needed in a learning management system.

Student View Mockups

Instructor View Mockups

Features

Novella allows students to access course material, submit assignments, work on quizzes, participate in polls, and view their own grades. The teacher's perspective of the app allows the teacher to facilitate and control the content that the student can access. Teachers may also take classroom attendance using the app.
