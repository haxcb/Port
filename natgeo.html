Getting people personally involved in the research and projects of National Geographic Explorers

Overview

Nat Geo Take Action is an application that allows users to personally interact with National Geographic Explorers by providing the opportunity for users to take action and get involved in the issues that they care about the most. The platform is designed to create greater depth and engagement between users and National Geographic Society content.

PROJECT GOAL
Create a personalized experience by allowing users to directly interact & fund explorers and projects

Explorers

The researchers and content creators who are the fundamental backbone of the National Geographic Society's content & findings.
We wanted to connect people directly with National Geographic Explorers & their projects to help people get involved and take action on all the issues that matter to them the most

Design Process

Why Crowdfunding?

As a non-profit, National Geographic Society relies on external donations to help fund the society's scientific endeavours. Like all non-profits, the question of bringing in more funding is always a cause of concern. When analyzing the society's old funding model, there was much left to be desired in the impersonal and multi-layered process.

Old Funding Model

We found an opportunity to reimagine how this process worked, by allowing for direct relationships between the National Geographic Explorers, their projects, and the users of the site. With this model, National Geographic Society would act as the umbrella under which these interactions would take place.
