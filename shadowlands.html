Captivating long time WoW fans with an uncharted world of new lore

Overview

World of Warcraft: Shadowlands is the 9th expansion for the massively popular World of Warcraft MMORPG. The announcement website was launched during BlizzCon and provides fans a first glimpse into the Shadowlands, the afterlife for all mortal souls in Warcraft lore.

PROJECT GOAL
Introduce previously unexplored lore to long time WoW fans
MY CONTRIBUTION

I worked closely with another designer for six months to propose the site strategy, create the wireframes, iterate on the style guide components, and refine the layout. I also proposed a number of motion explorations and worked closely with engineering to help realize some of these concepts.

A Closer Look at the Stats

On Team pages, fans can check in on how their favorite team is performing in the league.

On the Hero Stats page, HGC fans can gain insight on which heroes are strong in the current esports meta.

Design Process

Why fans love esports

Esports provides fans with an opportunity to engage with the game at a deeper level. Different fans have different motivations that drive them to consume esports.

Player Journey into Esports

With HGC, we focused on helping fans learn about the game and created opportunities for fans to follow their favorite teams and players closely.

Learn

Improving one's gameplay is a major motivator for engaging with esports. We addressed this need on the site by creating the Hero Stats page to highlight what the current meta is in HGC for players. A Heroes player can look at these stats as a point of reference in their own gameplay when picking/drafting heroes in game. For instance, a player sees that Muradin is currently the most picked hero in HGC and tries playing him in their own game to see what the hype is about.

Follow

When the site was originally created, we relied on the Standings page alone for users to access individual Team pages. After testing the site with users, we found that users needed an easier way to find individual teams, and thus we created a Teams page that lists all the different teams in their respective regions.
