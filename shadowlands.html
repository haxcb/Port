Captivating long time WoW fans with an uncharted world of new lore

Overview

World of Warcraft: Shadowlands is the 9th expansion for the massively popular World of Warcraft MMORPG. The announcement website was launched during BlizzCon and provides fans a first glimpse into the Shadowlands, the afterlife for all mortal souls in Warcraft lore.

PROJECT GOAL
Introduce previously unexplored lore to long time WoW fans
MY CONTRIBUTION

I worked closely with another designer for six months to propose the site strategy, create the wireframes, iterate on the style guide components, and refine the layout. I also proposed a number of motion explorations and worked closely with engineering to help realize some of these concepts.

A Closer Look at the Visual Style

One of the challenges of this project was visually encapsulating the varying themes in the afterlife. We iterated on the look and feel until we found the correct balance of gold, steel, damascus, and anima (blue soulstuff).

Design Process

Broaching the Unfamiliar

The Shadowlands expansion takes players into a never explored aspect of Warcraft lore - the afterlife. With the expansionâ€™s foreign setting, we had to be mindful of how we introduced new terminology on the page.

We realized the navigation was an area of the site that could confuse new users. The navigation is the first element a user would encounter on the page and it immediately presented the unfamiliar term, "covenants". A new user encountering the term might feel confused and jump to their own conclusions on what the term might mean.

Original Navigation

We went back to the drawing board with the navigation and came up with a more contextualized design. We grouped the page into two categories: world and features. Within features, we listed out our subpages and also added helpful descriptions for some of the new features. Our goal with the new design was to ensure that at the very least, users would know that a covenant is a major feature of the expansion and that it has subpages that go into a deeper dive.
