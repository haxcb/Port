Village

Breathing life into players' Collections

Overview

Hearthstone Mercenaries is a game mode where players build a team of six Mercenaries to take on Bounties and level up through combat. The Mercenary Village is the central hub within Mercenaries where a player begins their journey. As a player grows their Collection, their Village evolves to reflect their progress in the game. The Village also allows players to navigate to all of the features that make up the game mode.

PROJECT GOAL
Create a compelling visual representation of the player’s investment in Mercenaries.
MY CONTRIBUTION

I designed the end to end UI of the Village, including the Campfire, Travel Point, Workshop, and Training Grounds. I created flow diagrams, wireframes and prototypes. I gave art direction on final assets, and implemented the frontend visuals and logic in Unity.

A Closer Look at the Systems

A player’s Mercenaries will visit the Campfire in the Village with tasks to complete containing light story & dialogue.

The Workshop is another progression system in the Village that allows players to upgrade their buildings to unlock new functionality.

Design Process

Why Story­telling & Way­finding?

We identified two glaring issues that detracted from the average visitor's experience in the Louvre. Firstly, information about artwork was not easily understandable for most pieces beyond the museum's top three works (ie. Mona Lisa). Secondly, considering the massive size of the Louvre, navigating through the museum was a source of frustration for visitors.

With these two problems in mind, we decided to focus on building an in-museum experience to help visitors better understand pieces through storytelling, and supplemented this journey with an easier to use wayfinding system.
